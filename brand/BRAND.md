# TabGhost Brand Rules

## Name

- Primary name: TabGhost
- Product domain: TabGhost.lol
- Short form: TabGhost

## Brand intent

TabGhost is a browser workspace platform for agents, automation, QA, and privacy-conscious power users.

## Hard rules

- Do not change the project name without explicit maintainer approval.
- Do not change the logo, colours, or wordmark without explicit maintainer approval.
- Do not rename the GitHub organisation, owner, or repository namespace without explicit maintainer approval.
- Do not rewrite product positioning away from browser workspaces, session isolation, and automation.

## Voice

- Direct
- Technical
- Confident
- Open-source first
- No anti-detect framing
